Paludariums/Vivarium

The Paludarium is a living ecosystem where all of the living elements (mosses, plants, crabs, and fish) and non-living elements (the water, air, and rocks) interact with one another and are linked through nutrient cycles and energy flows. The balance of the ecosystem is supported each day with just a little food for the fish. A Paludarium is a type of vivarium that incorporates terrestrial and aquatic elements. Paludarium usually consists of an enclosed container in which organisms specific to the biome being simulated are kept.
